This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use RedzBot on Discord. 2. Information We Collect RedzBot may collect the following information: Discord User ID and Username: To track game scores and identify users. Server ID: To maintain server-specific points and leaderboards. Game Data: Points, trivia scores, and gameplay actions within the bot. 3. We do not share your personal information with third parties. 5. Data Retention Your data will remain stored as long as the bot is active on your server. Security We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated in the bot’s description or updates channel. 10.
